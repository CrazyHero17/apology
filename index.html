A message for Ayen

For Ayen

A confession, an apology, and a promise.

In every morning's dawn, you are the light,
My guiding dream throughout the darkest night.
Ayen, your beauty goes beyond the skin,
A steadfast heart, despite the wounds within.

You are complete, though you may stand alone,
You need nobody else, you hold your own.
So brave and strong, a beauty to behold,
One single smile brings peace, a joy untold.

Like sunflowers standing so tall and so bright,
You bloom in my world with a warm, yellow light.
Yet free as a dandelion caught in the breeze,
A resilient beauty that puts me at ease.

But I fell short, and I have made mistakes,
I am the reason why your own heart aches.
For all the promises I failed to keep,
And leaving you alone to hurt and weep.

When you were angry, fear would make me hide,
Instead of standing firmly by your side.
I dragged our problems out, made healing slow,
When you just needed me to not let go.

Forgive me for the damage I have done,
For all your tears, I am the guilty one.
It's not your fault your heart has grown so tired,
A love like yours was meant to be admired.

I love you, not because you love me too,
But just because you are Ayen, so true.
If given one more chapter, a new start,
I'd prove that I can love with all my heart.

And if I am too late to make things right,
My admiration stays, a guiding light.
For once you graced my life, so real and true,
A perfect Ayen, loved completely through.

"I am truly sorry for shutting down and not being there when you needed me the most. I meant every word of this poem. Take all the time you need. I'll be right here whenever you're ready to talk."
